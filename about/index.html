How It All Began

Born from a love of matcha and a desire to share that warmth with others, mofucha is a cozy home-based matcha business in Singapore, based right here in Clementi. What started as a simple appreciation for green tea slowly grew into a small space dedicated to crafting matcha drinks we're proud to serve.

Our journey began with countless experiments in our kitchen, perfecting the balance between the earthy depth of matcha and the creamy sweetness that makes each sip so satisfying. Today, we're proud to serve what many consider the best matcha latte in Clementi, Singapore.

Our Philosophy

We believe a good matcha drink should feel like a warm hug. Comforting, genuine, and made with care. Each cup is handcrafted using premium Japanese matcha, prepared fresh to bring out the smooth, balanced flavours of green tea in every sip.

At mofucha, we don't rush. We take our time to whisk each drink to perfection, ensuring that every customer receives a cup that we're genuinely proud to serve. It's this attention to detail that sets us apart.

The Name Behind the Brand

Our name combines "mofu mofu", meaning fluffy in Japanese, with "cha", meaning tea. It reflects our belief that every matcha latte should feel soft, comforting, and familiar, just like our little cat mascot who sits cosily inside a traditional matcha whisk.
