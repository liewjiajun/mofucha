Our Matcha Story

About mofucha

Born from a love of matcha and a desire to share that warmth with others, mofucha is a cozy home-based matcha business in Singapore, based right here in Clementi! What started as a simple appreciation for green tea slowly grew into a small space dedicated to crafting matcha drinks we're proud to serve, including what we hope are some of the best matcha latte drinks for everyday enjoyment.

We believe a good matcha drink should feel like a warm hug. Comforting, genuine, and made with care! Each cup is handcrafted using premium Japanese matcha, prepared fresh to bring out the smooth, balanced flavours of green tea in every sip.

Our name combines "mofu mofu", meaning fluffy in Japanese, with "cha", meaning tea! It reflects our belief that every matcha latte should feel soft, comforting, and familiar, just like our little cat mascot.
